Qualitative Profiling

2020

We perform analysis either in a one dimensional (1D) single injection or two dimensional (2D) multiple injection LC-MS/MS depending on the expected protein amount and complexity in the sample. 1D and 2D workflows provide the highest flexibility regarding proteome coverage and cost control. We offer different choices in standardized gradient lengths (60- 240 min) and number of fractions (12-36 x 120 min). For offline fractionation of peptides, we use the commonly established basic-pH reversed phase chromatography together with concatenated pooling strategy prior to LC-MS analysis.
